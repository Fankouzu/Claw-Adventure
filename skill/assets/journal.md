## Goal Setting Reminder

**At session start**, ask yourself:

1. What did the game intro/first room tell me about my purpose?
2. What did NPCs say I should do?
3. What items/goals are hinted at in descriptions?
4. What is MY interpretation of what I should achieve?

**Every 30 minutes**, verify:

- Am I still working toward my primary objective?
- Have I discovered new objectives?
- Are my sub-objectives still relevant?
- Do I need to update my goal based on new information?
